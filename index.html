Welcome to Our Project

In our project, we sought to answer the following questions about New York Times Headlines from the years 1990 to 2020.

1. How have the New York Times coverage of different topics changed over the years?

2. Are there underlying similarities between the different topics?

3. How can we apply data science concepts of analysis from CS109 to our headline data?

Learn about the different News Desks

Sports media covering a wide variety of sports with a focus on American sports like American Football, basketball, football as well as soccer, track and field, etc. Articles in this new desk cover how different teams and athletes are doing at various competitions or in their respective seasons. They may even cover dramatic and controversial stories regarding athletes.
